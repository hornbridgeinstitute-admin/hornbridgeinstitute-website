Our Mission
"When Africa learns, Africa leads."

HBI bridges critical gaps in education access, quality, and equity across the Horn of Africa — through evidence-based programs and partnerships aligned with the African Union's continental education agenda.

What We Do

Six Programs.
One Mission.

Every HBI program is designed to contribute measurably to the AU Decade of Education targets and Ethiopia's national education goals.

01

Foundational Learning Initiative

Accelerated literacy and numeracy programs for out-of-school children and adults through community learning centers.

Learn more →
02

Teacher Excellence Program

In-service teacher training, professional development, and classroom coaching across underserved regions.

Learn more →
03

Girls in School Initiative

Scholarships, menstrual health support, safe schools, and community engagement to keep girls learning.

Learn more →
04

Digital Learning & EdTech

ICT infrastructure, digital content in local Ethiopian languages, and teacher ICT training for rural schools.

Learn more →
05

TVET & Youth Skills

Market-linked vocational training for out-of-school youth aligned with Ethiopia's labor market needs.

Learn more →
06

Research & Policy

Education research publications and policy dialogues informing AU Commission and Ethiopian MoE decisions.
