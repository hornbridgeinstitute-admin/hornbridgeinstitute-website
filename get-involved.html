Institutional Partnerships

How we partner

1

Government Partnerships

HBI works with Ethiopia's Ministry of Education, Ministry of Labor and Skills, and regional education bureaus to align programs with national frameworks and support government school system strengthening.

2

UN & Multilateral Partnerships

HBI partners with UNICEF, UNESCO, and UNHCR on education programs reaching the most vulnerable populations, including conflict-affected children and refugees.

3

NGO Co-Implementation

HBI welcomes partnerships with other NGOs for joint program delivery, shared learning, and combined funding applications to major institutional donors.

4

Research & Academic Partnerships

HBI partners with universities, think tanks, and AU institutes including the Pan-African University for collaborative research and evidence generation.

5

Private Sector Partnerships

Technology companies, telecommunications firms, and Ethiopian businesses can partner with HBI on digital learning programs, skills development, and corporate social responsibility initiatives.
