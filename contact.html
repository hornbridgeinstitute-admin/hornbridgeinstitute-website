Frequently Asked

Common questions

Is HBI accepting grant applications?

HBI is currently seeking funding partners, not administering grants to others. We are building our program portfolio and welcome donor conversations.

How do I apply for HBI programs as a beneficiary?

HBI works through community and government channels. Contact our Ethiopia office or reach out via this form and we'll connect you with relevant program contacts.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

HBI is incorporated in the U.S. with 501(c)(3) status pending. Once approved, U.S. donations will be fully tax-deductible. Contact us for current donation arrangements.

Can diaspora community members get involved?

Absolutely — HBI was founded by a diaspora community member and deeply values the contribution of the Somali and Ethiopian diaspora. Contact us about how to engage.
